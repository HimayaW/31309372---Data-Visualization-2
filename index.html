GLOBAL ASYLUM SEEKERS (2006 - 2020)

This visualization shows the trends in global asylum data, which demonstrates from which countries most asylum seekers are from and which countries accept asylum seekers into there country along with rejections and complementary protections.

World Map showing the Country of Origin of most asylum seekers

It is clearly visible that there are higher number of applicants from Afghanistan, followed by Honduras in Central America and other countries like Colombia, Iraq and Nigeria. This changes over the period of 2006 to 2020.

Top 10 countries that accepted applicants

It can be seen that countries with asylum are dominated by most developed countries, the highest acceptance rate being Germany.

How has the provision of complementary protection changes over time?

Asylum seekers who have been accepted into the country but not being recognized come under complementary protection by the government of the specific country.During the time period after 2013, there has been significant changes in the number of applicants who have been given complementary protection.
